Welcome to DJI Flight Planner

This tool helps you create precise flight plans for your DJI Mini 4 Pro.

  • Start by clicking CREATE FLIGHT in the top menu bar.
  • Select Mapping or Inspection depending on your mission type.
  • Use DRAW to define your survey area.
  • Adjust Height, Speed, and Overlap in the left panel.
  • EDIT your polygon to see real-time path updates.
  • Export KMZ to import directly into the DJI Fly App.
